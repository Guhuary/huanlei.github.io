## Selected AI4Sci Research Projects

- **SurfDiff: Surface-Aware Protein-Ligand Docking** | 2023<br>
  Developed a surface-aware diffusion framework integrating surface-level binding-site identification, E(3)-equivariant pose refinement, and distance-likelihood-based ranking to improve docking accuracy and reduce steric clashes.
- **RC-DiffFlow: Reaction Coordinate Learning for Molecular Dynamics** | 2024<br>
  Developed an invertible framework combining data-dependent diffusion, normalizing flows, and Langevin dynamics to learn low-dimensional reaction coordinates and reduced molecular kinetics from molecular dynamics trajectories. This methodology later served as the foundation for the published JCTC work *Locating Transition States for Biomolecular Dynamics via Invertible Dimensionality Reduction*.
- **ForceFM: Force-Guided Flow Matching for Protein-Ligand Prediction** | 2025<br>
  Developed a force-guided flow-matching framework that incorporates intermolecular force information into protein-ligand structure prediction, improving docking accuracy and physical plausibility. Accepted by NeurIPS 2025.
- **Hierarchical Molecular Manifold Learning for Rapid Flexible Docking** | 2025 - Present<br>
  Developing a regression-based framework for efficient flexible docking by jointly modeling ligand and protein conformational adjustments.
- **Fast Molecular Conformation Optimization via Latent Drifting** | 2025 - Present<br>
  Developing latent-space drifting methods for efficient molecular conformation optimization and iterative structural refinement.
